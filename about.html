About me

My name's Jason and I'm from Joliet, Illinois. Growing up I was always extremely curious and this curiousity eventually focused on technology. I was always intersted by technology and I grew an affinity for it. That made the de facto computer guy of the house, tasked with fixing the computer whenever it wasn't working. I went to college and declared my major as computer science and initially I didn't know where in the field I wanted to end up after graduation. I just knew I wanted to learn how to code and become really good at it. At an internship I worked I was able to work on a project where I made a front end for the company's internal videos on their intranet. I loved it and from then on I knew I wanted to be a web developer. My curiousity is still going strong and it makes me want to know everything I can about my field which is why I want to eventually become a full-stack web developer. I know I have a lot to learn and I always will but I'm ready to start learning and to start my career.
